About Me

Ania Bilek, a warm form glass artist, creates one-of-a-kind jewelry and functional glass décor.

This passion for glass has unexpected changed Ania’s life. A few years ago her appreciation for the arts finally drew her into unique art glass form creations. Glass is fascinating to Ania for its simplicity of structure, functionality, durability and most of all, the unbelievable beauty of colors.

Ania is constantly developing new techniques and designs. She has been lucky find people with the same passion about glass that each and mentor her. She is always searching to find her own way of expressing life through art and finding uniqueness and simplicity in her creations. Every piece has a story and everyone who is supporting her in her journey is special.

Ania makes jewelry using glass beads designed and created by her. As well, she likes to incorporate high quality materials such as gemstones, Swarovski crystals, sterling silver, and gold. Her pieces have been created to compliment each of her customers’ personality, spirit of the moment, and appreciation of present.

All the work featured on this website has been hand-crafted by Ania. Please enjoy her designs. She is looking forward to sharing her art with you.
